About us

Speed Up Sports is not just a brand. It’s a platform for kids to reach out into the vast world of sports. Pick Football, Cricket, Boxing, Badminton or Table Tennis, we’ve got them covered. It provides these sports enthusiasts, who just want to play anywhere, anytime, with a variety of sporting goods/ sporting toys which would definitely build their game and give them a head start to pursue the sport they love. ​


Speed Up Sports brand was formed in 1997, directly under Brightway Exports International, to look out and cater to a different and our favorite class of audience , children.
